Paper

- [[Paper](https://arxiv.org/abs/2512.21257)] ReaSeq from Taobao. Recommendation
  systems based on in-log IDs are limited by (1) the sparsity of co-occurrence
  statistical signals and (2) the invisibility of user behavior outside logs.
  This work attempts to solve the first problem with an LLM reasoning setup to
  automatically tag items, and the second problem with a generation-based
  augmentation of the user action sequence.
